Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun, as well as the largest of the terrestrial planets of the solar system in terms of radius, mass and density of matter. Earth is also known as 'blue planet'. The earth was discovered by Galileo Galilei and proved that the earth is spherical.

Distance: 1AU (149,6 MILLION KM)

Orbit: 365,24 DAYS

Diameter: 12.756 KM

Atmosphere: -13°C TO 37°C
